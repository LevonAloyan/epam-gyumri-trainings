A river is a natural flowing watercourse,
usually freshwater, flowing towards an
ocean, sea, lake or another river.
 A lake is an area filled with
water, localized in a basin,
surrounded by land. A waterfall is a point in a river or
stream where water flows over a
vertical drop or a series of steep
drops.
